About Us

We are a team of 6 students who are making a incubator. The team consists of 6 people where three are mechanical engineers. The other three are embedded system engineers. We were given the task to make a incubator that is lighter, has online accsess and does the same job as a incubator that is used now. On the other hand we had to make it on a low budget
